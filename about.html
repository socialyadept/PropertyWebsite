Introduction

The firm Nabi Buksh & Sons was originally founded in 1930 by a leading agriculturist Raja Nabi Buksh hailing from the suburban Rawalpindi (present-day sector I-8, Islamabad) primarily as a horse-trading company catering the cavalry needs of the British Indian Army.Apart from providing contingents to the cavalry department, the firm also provided lands on lease to the British Raj in Rawalpindi for military barracks and depots known as Ojhri camp. The said lease agreement was further continued with Pakistan Army after 1947.

Over the years the company has developed into one of the leading enterprises and has worked within the different segments of the society to provide assistance in better living and sustainability. Over the years we have worked hard to stay innovative, true to our core values and become one of the pioneers of construction industry.

In the past we focused solely on governmental and military contracts for development nationwide. However, in the recent years we have taken our nearly 75 years of experience into private sector. Our team employs highly experienced and veteran civil, electrical, mechanical and architectural engineers who understand and share our values and beliefs of providing unparalleled innovation and dedication to the industry.
